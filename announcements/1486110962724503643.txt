## 📉 INITIALIZING `pricePulse`
Hi @everyone. The days of hoarding your cubes for *static & predictable store prices are officially over*. We are injecting a little real-world chaos into the mainframe. 
### 💸  your new source of financial anxiety & inventory FOMO.
**The store is now tethered to market forces. **The system will automatically adjust the cube value of all rewards based on what they are currently worth "in the wild". Here is how you play the market *(or how the market plays you)*:
## 🤑 *Time is Cubes*
- once per day, each reward will be adjusted by (+/-) 1024 zCubes to simulate real world markets
  - if supply lines dry, values soar... cubes will go up up and away 
  - if the value tanks, the cubes will fall, get it before someone else does
  - If an item sits on the shelf too long, it starts to rot & its value slashed *10% more each week*
- **proTips for winning** at `pricePulse`: 
  - Do you want to play this ? ... now ? 
  - Will it be ⬆️ or ⬇️ tomorrow ?
  - Should you wait 1 more day or 1 more week ?
  - ||Can you live with the butthurt if someone else snags it ?||
-# **(Mandatory Legal Disclaimer** before the OSC kicks down the server rack: __CUBES HAVE ABSOLUTELY ZERO REAL-WORLD VALUE__. You cannot pay your rent in cubes. This entire system is just a brutal, elaborate simulation of real-world supply & demand designed purely for our collective amusement. Welcome to the meta-game.)

The market is live, all reward values adjusted daily (+/-1024)
