## ⚖️ BALANCE FOR FUN: THE SPEED PROTOCOL
-# The rebalance is here. @everyone **The mission: Twice as easy to win... half**.
By making all game loops half as long (and winnings half as much), we can play twice as much. Now you win ||roughly the same amount|| <:zCube:1439260731399733418>  **twice the speed.**
## 🕹️ __HARD MODE__ ENGAGED
- **DailyDose** __HardMode__ 
  - More risk, more reward. Still once every 25 hours, but now limited to 2 vouchers per 48hr stream ~~(was 3)~~.
  - **Critical**: If you miss your cooldown, you'll need to wait until the next stream starts. No exceptions.
- **VodVote** __HardMode__: 
  - Twice as much fun and more chances to win. 
  - Cooldowns are halved and rewards are halved. 
  - Play twice as much... double the chance to win (...half).
- **PixelPower** __HardMode__: 
  - Recalibrated for pleasure and dopamine. 
  - Now featuring 12 rounds of 3 words every - 1 word per person, per round. ~~(was 6x6/2pp)~~
  - ~20 minutes between ~~(was 40)~~
  - No more hourly limit. Since it’s ~3 rounds per hour at 1 word each, you literally can't hit a limit anymore.
  - To keep the economy stable at this new speed, **X & Z have been retired from scoring.** 
    - Use them for the art, but don't expect a payout for "farming" the ends of the alphabet.

-# To keep the gears turning smoothly, the system is now taxing "noise."
- **3 attempts without a valid dictionary word, or spamming words when past the limit.**
  - 🟨 Yellow Card now make next word worth 50%
  - 🟨+🟨 = 🟥 (2 Yellow = Red): Your next word is worth exactly 7 <:zCube:1439260731399733418>

Play fair, watch the rounds fly, and avoid the Noise Tax.
