## 📺 VODVOTE 2.0: THE OPEN DOOR POLICY
-# Hi @everyone The era of choosing between watching or voting is over. *We’re multitasking now*.
## 🔄 THE NEW FLOW
- **Vote While You Watch**: You can now cast your vote at any time while watching a VOD
- **The Flip**: `!v###` The command to ~~start~~ __end__ a round
  - **now reversed!! it will end the current voting round** 
    - reveals if anyone guessed correctly this round.
    - start playing the popular choice - most selected last round 
- **One Shot**: You get one vote per round. Use it wisely.
## 🟨 THE DOUBLE-DIP PENALTY
-# Since the doors never close, some of you will try to spam the ballot box. Don't.
- 🟨 Yellow Card: Voting twice in the same round triggers a warning.
- 🟨 + 🟨 = 🟥 Red Card: 2 yellow cards and your next entry is worth exactly 7 cubes.
-# The system is simpler, faster, and stays open for business 24/7. 
Just don't trip on the way in.
## 🧾  TLDR: 
Vote anytime during a VOD. You only get one vote. Double-voting earns a Yellow Card; two yellows equal a red & 7 cubes on your next turn.
