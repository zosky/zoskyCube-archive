## 📜 PIXEL POWER 2.0: EFFICIENCY PROTOCOL
Look, @everyone  I don’t like getting pinged this much, and I definitely don’t like being the one doing the pinging. But the system is evolving, so here we grow 🤡 ...
-# Enjoy your <:zCube:1439260731399733418> for reacting—if you care about what’s changed, keep reading. If you don't, react anyway for your 250 <:zCube:1439260731399733418>.
## 🎨 Pixel Power 2.0
-# The art engine is getting a major overhaul. It’s bigger, more structured, and less chaotic.
- **Expanded Capacity**: Increased to 36 words per game (was 32).
- **The Round System**:  6 rounds × 6 words each. __2 per person each round__
- **The Letter Constraint**: Each round will now pick a specific letter. Every word submitted must begin with that letter.
- **Persistence**: A round remains open until all 6 slots are filled. The next round will begin in 15–20 minutes.
- **Personal Limits**: You are still limited to 12 words max per day, but this is now clearly visible under the art and in the chat responses.
## 🚫 The "Break" Protocol (Timeouts)
-# Ignoring the state of the game is a sign of disrespect toward the system and other players. If you choose to ignore the live data, the system will reward you with a mandatory break.
- You will receive a timeout for:
  - Sending words that start with the wrong letter.
  - Sending words between rounds when the system is closed.
  - Sending words after you have exhausted your daily limit.
- **__All timeouts can be easily avoided if you play the game__** 
  - look at the state of the game on the liveStream
  - head the warnings provided in the chat
  - Read the screen, follow the rules, win more cubes

**TLDR**: Pixel Power is now 36 words across 6 letter-specific rounds. Your daily limit (12) is now visible. Ignoring the current letter or round status results in a timeout.
