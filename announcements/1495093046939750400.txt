> You choose the suffering; I just provide the commentary and the cubes <:zCube:1439260731399733418> ...
### 🛂  how we play:
- **every valid vote is winning +33 <:zCube:1439260731399733418> **
- Use `!r(...)` to nominate a game next to enter the gauntlet
  - **Optional**: Add a vote count **max 4 per game** e.g., `!r(...) 4`
- quota: "The SteamGifts Method"
  - Every user has a 25-vote maximum.
  - 🍄 regenerate +1 votes per hour.
-# vote spam will be ignored. no cubes & longer to regenerate
### 🥇  The W:
- top-voted game will be played live "next time"
- winning game must have at least 11+ votes from 3+ users.
- each round lasts 24 hours if any game qualifies for the W, or another 24h until 1 does.
- Each game is locked for 4min before it can be voted again.
- i have the final say b/c sometime life (or adulting) happens
