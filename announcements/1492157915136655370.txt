## 📢 A Quick Note on DailyDose ... and Overdosing
-# Hi everyone, I’m not going to ping updates every day, and let’s be real: the folks this message is intended for are likely not going to see it anyway. However, 
to keep things fair for those of you playing the right way, we are implementing two new rules for the DailyDose system. **These won't affect anyone playing properly**, but they are here to put a stop to any "overdosing" attempts.
### 🛡️ The Anti-Overdose Protocol
Attempting to claim your dose too early will now trigger a cooldown penalty. The math is simple:
- **Reset**: If you try to claim **while you have <20 hours remaining** your cooldown is **reset to 25 hours** ... better luck next time.
- **Heavy**: If you try to claim **while you still have >20 hours remaining**, your cooldown will be **extended to 75hrs**... maybe read the rules ?
### 📖  TLDR
The goal here is balance. If you're playing the game as intended, you have nothing to worry about. 
If you're trying to game the system, the system is now gaming back.

Carry on
~ z
