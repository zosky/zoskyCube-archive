## 🖋️ PIXELPOWER v3: THE CINEMATIC EVOLUTION
-# @everyone One frame was never going to be enough to contain the collective chaos of this community. We’re officially moving from "single-image snapshots" to full-scale visual storytelling.
Introducing *The Comic Update*. We’re not just making art anymore; we’re directing movies on a 2x2 grid.
## 🎞️ THE NEW FORMAT: PANEL BY PANEL
The canvas has evolved. Instead of one crowded image, the bot now acts as a *Lead Storyboard Artist*, taking your input & slicing it into a 4-part narrative arc.
- **Layout**: A clean 2x2 comic strip
- **Narrative**: The bot will now interpret your words through four stages:
  - Setup - Conflict - Climax - and Resolution.
- **Visual Consistency**: The system will lock in a Protagonist with distinct traits to ensure the story actually makes sense from Panel 1 to Panel 4. No dialogue bubbles—we’re letting the art do the talking.
## ⚙️ THE UPGRADE: 5-WORD PAYLOAD
To fuel this new storytelling engine, we’re increasing the complexity of the rounds.
- **Quantity**: We’ve bumped it from 3 words per round to 5 words. 
- **The Master Prompt**: A full game now generates 60 Seed Words (12 rounds, no change there).
- **The Distribution**: Each panel will be powered by 15 unique words contributed by the community.
## 🛠️ Z’S NOTE ON QUALITY
This is a high-performance engine. If you feed it "garbage-tier" words, our comic is going to look like a fever dream in a junkyard. If you want epic 4-panel masterpieces, give the Creative Director something to work with. I’ve updated the logic to handle the storyboard transitions, so don’t break the machine. I’m already annoyed enough at how much processing power this is going to eat.

TLDR: pixelPower is now a 4-panel comic generator. 5 words per round, 60 words total per game. Play the story, don't just farm the points.
