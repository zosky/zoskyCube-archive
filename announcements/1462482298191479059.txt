## 🎨 PIXEL POWER 2.1: QUALITY OVER QUANTITY
-# @everyone A wise man once said `!art fart`. Not only is it fun to say, the [resulting render](https://discord.com/channels/1360087703286186116/1439051254541062305/1462077999741665404) was epic. This is the core of why we are tweaking pixelPower once more. This is a word game and an art project, not a point-farming exercise. From now on, the system will reward those who contribute to the aesthetic.
## 💎 THE QUALITY BONUS
-# Short, punchy, and visual words are the lifeblood of great renders.
- **1.5x Bonus: Any word between 4–7 letters (with no other penalties) **
## 📉 THE TAX MANIFESTO
-# If you are hunting for points by padding words or using clinical jargon that the AI can't visualize, you are going to pay for it. 
The following taxes now apply:
- -80% Medical Suffixes *( -itis, -osis, -ectomy ... )*
- -70% Hyphenated Words *( multi-layered, self-aware )*
- -60% Compound-Exploit / "un-" Prefix *( unhappy, unreal, over-words )*
- -50% Plural-Padding / "-ly" Suffix *( Adding 's' or 'ly' just for length )*
- -50% Word Length > 12 *( Stop writing novels in a single slot )*
- -40% Lazy Suffixes *(-ness, -ment, -tion)*
- -30% Adverb/Adjective Inflation *(bloating the words unnecessarily)*
## 👁️ VISUALIZE OR FAIL
Words like "adjudicate" cannot be visualized well. If the prompt can't "see" it, you aren't playing the game; you're just clicking buttons.Think about what makes an epic image and take pride in what you add to the community art. 
- **If you continue to hunt for points via word-bloat, you will not have fun**. 
- **If you provide quality, the renders will be epic—and so will your points.**
## TLDR: 
Short, visual words (4-7 letters) get a 1.5x boost. 
Lazy padding, prefixes, and medical jargon get taxed up to 80%. 
Play for the art, not the farm.

~ z
