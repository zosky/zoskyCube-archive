-# Hi @everyone. Whether you're in Canada, have family nearby, or are just here for the vibes, today is Family Day.

- **The Grind**: It was essentially invented because the stretch between Christmas and March Break is a cold, dark void of endless work. We needed a pit stop.
- **The Goal**: It’s a day meant for togetherness—or at least a day where you aren’t legally required to answer emails.
- **The Family**: Whether they are across the room or across the ocean, today we celebrate the people who haven't blocked our numbers yet.
