## 📅 THE PERSONAL CLOCK
-# Your "day" and your "pace" are now yours alone.
- **Always Open**: letter-specific rounds will now be 2–3 minutes long. **no cooldown**
- **12 Word Limit**: __12 words per "day"__ (no change there)
- **Your Daily Reset**: __personal  25hr timer__ starts with your 1st word
- **Your Heartbeat**: __add another word every ~22-28-minutes__ 
  - or more or less, its up to you really... 
## 📈 THE CARROT
-# If you can manage a basic schedule, get ready to win more cubes...
- **The Streak**: consecutive turns will begin a streak
- **The Bonus**: stacking **+10%**  (max of 50%).
- **The Pause**: If you hit your daily limit, your streak pauses.
- **The Reset**: on "your next-turn" (after your cooldown), if you miss 3 letter rounds
    - Your streak dies. Start over.
## 📉 THE STICK
-# If you can’t read a clock, the system will start harvesting your cubes.
- **The "I Can't Wait" Tax**: 
  - adding words during your cooldown, (or past your daily limit) will trigger a __stacking 50% penalty.__ 
    - once: Half score
    - twice: Quarter score
    - ... It gets ugly fast.
-# - **Power Player Protocol**: If you’re a 12-word-a-day addict - remember, you will need to play in 1 of the first 3 rounds for each new picture & 1 more turn to keep your streak going.
## TLDR: 
12 words on a personal 25h reset timer. 22-minute personal cooldown between words. Play within 3 rounds of your cooldown ending for up to a +50% bonus. Spamming during cooldown = stacking 50% penalty.
