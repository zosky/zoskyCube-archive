- **The Long Game**: Now a Best of 5 *(or 3 lives)*. This might actually give some of us a fighting chance against Avon.

- **Wall Clearance**: Like mini-golf, if you spawn hugging a wall, you will be nudged away. No more "stuck" starts.

  - **Result**: **__An immediate 2-hour timeout__**. *
